Rice Recipes

Description of the Recipe

The dish centers on a fragrant rice base made from long-grain rice that has been cooked and stir-fried with finely chopped vegetables such as carrots, green peas, sweet corn, and green beans. Its warm yellow-gold hue points to the use of curry powder or turmeric, hallmarks of West African–style fried rice. The rice is further enriched with aromatic seasonings—garlic, onions, curry, thyme, and possibly stock or seasoning cubes—resulting in a deeply savory, well-rounded flavor profile.

Complementing the rice are generous pieces of chicken, most likely thighs or quarters, that have been carefully marinated and then roasted or grilled until well browned. The cooking method produces a crisp, flavorful exterior while preserving a juicy, tender interior. A light garnish of fresh herbs, such as mint or parsley, crowns the dish, adding a fresh aroma and a touch of visual brightness that balances the richness of the meal.

Ingredients

  • Long-grain rice
  • Carrots (finely chopped)
  • Green peas
  • Sweet corn
  • Green beans
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Curry powder
  • Turmeric (optional, for color)
  • Thyme
  • Chicken (thighs or quarters)
  • Stock or seasoning cubes
  • Fresh herbs (mint or parsley for garnish)

Steps for making the dish

  1. Cook the rice according to package instructions.
  2. Marinate the chicken with curry powder, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Roast or grill the chicken until well browned and cooked through.
  4. Stir-fry the vegetables in a pan with garlic and onions until tender.
  5. Add the cooked rice and stir-fried vegetables to the pan.
  6. Season with turmeric and stock or seasoning cubes.
  7. Garnish with fresh herbs before serving.
