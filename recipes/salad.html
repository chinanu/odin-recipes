Salad Recipe

Description of the Recipe

This refreshing salad combines crisp lettuce with a variety of fresh vegetables and a tangy vinaigrette. It's a perfect light meal or side dish that's both healthy and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce (romaine or butter lettuce)
  • Tomatoes (cherry or beefsteak)
  • Cucumber (sliced)
  • Red onion (thinly sliced)
  • Feta cheese (crumbled)
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Dried oregano or basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps for making the dish

  1. Wash and dry the lettuce leaves thoroughly.
  2. Chop all vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the lettuce and all vegetables.
  5. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat evenly.
  6. Sprinkle crumbled feta cheese on top before serving.
